Sheet Pan Buttermilk Pancakes


Ingredients
  • cooking spray
  • 3 cups baking mix
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Line a sheet pan (approx. 13 x18 inches) or jelly roll pan (12x17 inches) with parchment paper.
  3. Combine baking mix, buttermilk, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon in a large mixing bowl
  4. Mix until the ingredients are well combined, but don't overmix. Pour batter onto the parchment paper-lined pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden, 15 to 18 minutes.
  6. Remove sheet pan from the oven. Lift the parchment paper with the pancake from the pan and set it onto a cutting board
  7. Slice the pancake sheet into squares using a pizza cutter or into your desired shapes with a cookie cutter.
