Odin Recipes

Kurze


Ingredients
  • Beef - 300 g
  • Onion - 2 pcs.
  • Greens - 1 bunch.
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Dumpling dough - 500 g
Steps
  1. Knead the dough for dumplings.
  2. pass greens and onions through a meat grinder
  3. roll out the finished dough with a thickness of 2-3 mm,
  4. cut circles with a diameter of 6-8 cm.
  5. Tilt the tip down towards the stuffing.
  6. Dumpling dough - 500 g
  7. cook in salted, boiling water for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Put on a dish and brush with butter.
